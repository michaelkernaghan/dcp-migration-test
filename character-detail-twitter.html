Character Profile

TwitterProd is an experimental account representing the "social influencer" crowd. Instead of a traditional phone number, it uses the handle @standardtesting on X (formerly Twitter).

This account focuses on social media integrations—posting updates, bug reports, and test findings. It skips ID verification and 2FA, making it a bare-bones but convenient test user.

Most of its activity involves filing bug reports and retweeting official platform news. The account actively engages with the DCP community through the Discord portal, providing feedback and participating in discussions about platform features and issues.

Social Auth Note: This account tests the social authentication pathway and phone-only registration flow during migration. Discord portal access is essential and must be maintained during migration.
