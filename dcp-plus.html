DCP+

A blockchain-enabled, multi-device streaming platform that lets studios and independent creators upload film & TV content, sell it to viewers in four monetization modes, and pay out royalties or review rewards automatically in TALNT tokens.

Netflix-class Experience Crypto Rails Multi-Device TALNT Rewards

Product Vision

What DCP+ Is

Viewers get a Netflix-class experience on web, mobile and smart-TV while the business keeps crypto rails, license enforcement and audit logs completely behind the scenes.

Key Value Propositions
  • Four Monetization Modes: Free, Ad-supported, Subscription, Transactional
  • Automatic Payouts: Royalties and review rewards in TALNT tokens
  • Multi-Device Support: Web, mobile, and smart-TV platforms
  • Blockchain Transparency: Immutable audit logs and license enforcement

Technical Components

Smart Contract Layer
TALNT Net (Base L2 + AppChain)
  • Immutable Wallets: Secure user identity and asset ownership
  • Automated Payments: Royalty distribution and TALNT token transfers
  • Review Reward Escrow: Transparent reward system for content engagement
  • License Enforcement: Immutable content rights and usage tracking
Media Pipeline
AWS MediaConvert → S3 → MediaPackage (+DRM) → CloudFront
  • Adaptive Renditions: Multiple quality levels for different devices
  • Multi-DRM: Just-in-time DRM key generation and delivery
  • Global CDN: Worldwide content delivery with low latency
  • Performance Optimization: Efficient encoding and delivery pipeline
